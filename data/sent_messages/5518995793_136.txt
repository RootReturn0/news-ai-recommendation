Usage Guide

What this bot can do:
- Save your preferred topics and keywords
- Search personalized news with /news
- Show trending stories with /hotnews
- Update your keywords automatically from feedback on sent news

Command examples:
/topics AI, startups
/keywords OpenAI, YC
/settings
/news today
/hotnews

Feedback:
- Reply /like or react with 👍 to add related keywords
- Reply /dislike or react with 👎 to remove related keywords
